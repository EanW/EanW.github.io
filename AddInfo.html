A Little more about Me

I plan on using my knowledge in Graphic Design to not only get me a career that I'll enjoy, but to be able to use my creativity for personal use as well. Such as creating company logos and websites for friends and family. I see that as an opportunity to improve my skills and learn something in the process. No two logos, images, or websites are the same to me. My ultimate goal is to be able to sustain myself in this field by being able to work at home and make images or websites for clients. I hope that you've learned a thing or two about me personally, as well as my goals involving Graphic Design.

Thank you for visiting my site!
