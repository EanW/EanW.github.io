Who am I?

As you might have guessed by now, my name is Ean White. I am 22 years old and aspiring to be a Multimedia Graphic Designer. What first got me interested in Graphic Design was the world of video games. When I was 13 I got my first Xbox 360 and that was when I really started to enjoy playing all kinds of different games and to some extent, I'm still that way today. I always thoguht that I would want to make my own video games. However, I've somewhat broadened that idea now by wanting to do more than just participate in creating video games. I want to be a Graphic Designer, and that means being proficient in other areas.
